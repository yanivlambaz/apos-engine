# SERVICE PAGE SYSTEM

Document ID: EXEC-110

Version: 1.0

Status: APPROVED

Classification: MASTER SPECIFICATION

Priority: CRITICAL

---

# Purpose

A Service Page is not a sales page.

It is an educational decision-support document that enables visitors to confidently choose a professional service.

Every Service Page exists to become the most complete resource available for one specific service.

A Service Page should answer every important question before asking the visitor to contact the business.

---

# Primary Objectives

Every Service Page must achieve all of the following:

• Explain the service

• Build trust

• Reduce uncertainty

• Demonstrate expertise

• Support SEO

• Support AI Search

• Generate qualified leads

---

# User Intent

Every Service Page serves exactly one primary search intent.

Examples:

Private Nurse

Night Nurse

Post Surgery Care

IV Therapy

Tax Refund for Employees

Capital Gains Tax Refund

Business Tax Consultation

Never mix multiple services into one page.

# Hero

Must contain:

Short explanation

Primary CTA

Secondary CTA

Professional image

Trust statement

Maximum one screen height.

---

# Quick Summary

Purpose:

Allow visitors to understand the service in less than one minute.

Contents:

Definition

Who needs it

Typical situations

Expected outcome

Suitable candidates

---

# When This Service Is Needed

Purpose:

Help users recognize themselves.

Examples:

Symptoms

Situations

Events

Problems

Use bullet lists.

Avoid long paragraphs.

---

# Who Is This Service For

Clearly define:

Suitable users

Unsuitable users

Alternative solutions

Never recommend a service when another option is more appropriate.

Trust increases conversion.

---

# Benefits

Every benefit must follow:

Benefit

↓

Explanation

↓

Practical impact

Never list features without explaining why they matter.

---

# Service Process

Visitors should know exactly what happens.

Recommended structure:

Contact

↓

Assessment

↓

Scheduling

↓

Service Delivery

↓

Follow-up

Maximum:

6 steps.

---

# Pricing

Purpose:

Reduce pricing anxiety.

If exact pricing cannot be displayed:

Explain pricing factors.

Provide ranges when possible.

Never hide pricing unnecessarily.

Transparency increases trust.

---

# FAQ

Minimum:

12 questions.

Recommended:

20+

Questions should address:

Duration

Eligibility

Preparation

Risks

Benefits

Availability

Common concerns

Alternatives

Schema required.

# AI Requirements

The page should answer:

What is this service?

Who needs it?

Who does not?

How does it work?

What are the benefits?

What are the alternatives?

How much does it cost?

What happens next?

Never force AI systems to infer missing information.

# Content Quality Standard

Every Service Page should be considered complete only if it answers every meaningful question a visitor is likely to ask before making contact.

If unanswered questions remain, the page is incomplete.
