# Purpose

This document defines the architecture of an Authority Platform.

It does not describe a website.

It describes a digital authority ecosystem.

Every APOS implementation must follow this model.

---

# Definition

An Authority Platform is a knowledge-driven digital business designed to become the most trusted source in a specific market while consistently generating qualified business opportunities.

The platform exists to solve user problems.

Lead generation is the consequence of trust.

Never the objective.

---

# Platform Objectives

Every APOS platform must accomplish all of the following.

Educate users.

Answer questions.

Build authority.

Build trust.

Generate qualified leads.

Support AI search.

Support organic search.

Scale without architectural changes.

# Knowledge-Centric Architecture

Traditional websites organize information by pages.

APOS organizes information by knowledge.

Pages are only one representation of knowledge.

Knowledge always comes first.

---

# Entity-Based Architecture

Business

Service

Article

Guide

Question

Answer

Review

City

Category

Every entity must have relationships.

Nothing exists in isolation.
