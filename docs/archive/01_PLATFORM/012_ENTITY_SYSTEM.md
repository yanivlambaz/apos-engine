# Purpose

Every APOS project is built from entities.

Pages do not represent the business.

Entities represent the business.

Pages are generated by connecting entities together.

The Entity System is the foundation of every APOS implementation.

---

# Definition

An Entity is a real-world object, concept, person, service, location or idea that has independent meaning.

Examples:

Business

Service

Treatment

Tax Refund

Tax Credit

City

Article

Guide

FAQ

Review

Author

Medical Condition

Tax Form

Legal Right

Insurance Provider

Government Office

Every project consists of entities.

Never pages.

---

# Core Rule

Pages are temporary.

Entities are permanent.

Never model the system around pages.

Always model the system around entities.

---

# Anti-Patterns

Do not create page-only content.

Do not duplicate entities.

Do not create disconnected entities.

Do not create entities without relationships.

Do not create relationships without meaning.

---

# Success Criteria

An APOS project is successful when every important business concept exists as an entity before it exists as a page.

Pages are generated from entities.

Authority grows through entities.

Search engines understand entities.

AI systems understand entities.

Users benefit from entities.

Everything starts with entities.
